HETVC Core
Pre-call hours — Counted from the crew member's personal call time, not unit call. Must be approved on the call sheet. Pre-call time is always the crew member's paid start.
Camera wrap vs unit wrap — Camera crew work to their own wrap (last camera out), not unit wrap. Camera wrap triggers OT calculation for the camera department.
Meal penalties — Flat £35 per breach, due at the 6-hour point from call. Penalty does not stop the clock — further penalties accrue each subsequent 6-hour period without a meal.
OT on 6th & 7th day — OT tiers still apply on top of the day premium. A 7th day at 13+ hours = base rate × 2.0 × 1.5 OT multiplier, effectively ×3.0 the basic hourly rate.
HP on all variable earnings — Holiday pay accrues on basic + OT (2302 + 2360). The HP uplift always posts to nominal 2302 regardless of which earnings type generated it.
NIC secondary threshold — Employer NIC at 13.8% applies only above £967/week. Weeks below this threshold incur no employer NIC.

Holiday Pay Treatment UK
This is a critical accounting decision that affects how the cost report reads. Check the offer letter carefully — many deals specify this explicitly.
Exclusive of Holiday Pay
The quoted rate does NOT include holiday pay. HP is calculated on top as an additional 12.07% uplift and posted separately to nominal 2302. The crew member receives more than the stated rate.
£550/day + HP → £616.39 total
Inclusive of Holiday Pay
The quoted rate already includes holiday pay. The basic element is back-calculated: rate ÷ 1.1207. The crew member receives exactly the stated rate — HP is embedded.
£550/day all-in → £490.76 basic + £59.24 HP
