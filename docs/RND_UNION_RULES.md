# R&D: Union Rules — Turnaround, Consecutive Days, Payslips, Contracted Hours, Receipts

## A. TURNAROUND RULES

| Union | Min Turnaround | Studio vs Location | Measured From | Penalty | Forced Call |
|-------|---------------|-------------------|---------------|---------|-------------|
| **IATSE (US)** | 10hrs (Camera DP/Op: 11hrs studio, 10hrs distant) | Yes — differs | Company Wrap + 1hr (NOT personal release) | Continuous employment if <5hrs rest. If invaded >1hr but 5+ hrs, hours added to previous shift for OT calc | After 2 consecutive 14+ hr days with rest invasion: return at rate at dismissal + 1hr ST |
| **SAG-AFTRA (US)** | 12hrs studio (reducible to 10hrs exterior, 11hrs overnight) | Yes | Personal dismissal (NOT company wrap) | Day Players: daily rate or $900 per violation. Weekly: one day's pay or $950 | 56hr weekly rest (reducible to 54hrs if Mon call ≥ 6AM) |
| **DGA (US)** | 9hrs daily, 54hrs weekend | No | Wrap | 1/3 of OT rate per hour of invasion, quarter-hour increments | UPM 16+ hrs: additional day's salary per 4-hr period beyond 16 |
| **Teamsters 399 (US)** | 8hrs basic rest | 54hrs after 5 days, 32hrs after 6 days | Wrap | 3× hourly rate for invaded hours | 2.5× after 14hrs, 3× after 15hrs |
| **PACT/BECTU (UK)** | 11hrs (legal requirement) | No difference | Release | TV: 0.5T per 15-min increment (total 1.5T). Film MMP: 1.5T per 30-min increment | Crew cannot be compelled to break turnaround (H&S) |
| **Ver.di (Germany)** | 11hrs mandatory, NO exception | No | Release | PROHIBITED — cannot schedule violation | N/A — simply illegal |
| **CCNPC (France)** | 11hrs (reducible to 9hrs with convention) | No | Release | Per labour code | 48hrs weekly rest including Sunday |
| **MEAA (Australia)** | 10-12hrs (varies by agreement) | Varies | Release | Penalty rates per MPPA | Standard 10hr day, 50hr week |

### Critical Distinction: IATSE measures from COMPANY WRAP, SAG-AFTRA from PERSONAL DISMISSAL

## B. WEEK BOUNDARY TURNAROUND

**All unions: YES, turnaround carries across week boundaries.**

- Penalty applied to the **current/new week** (the day the violation occurs)
- If previous week's payroll is locked, penalty does NOT retroactively amend it
- Payroll bureaus (Cast & Crew, EP, Sargent-Disc) track this automatically

**SAG-AFTRA:** 56-hour weekly rest period explicitly spans week boundaries.
**IATSE:** 54-hour weekend turnaround (5-day) or 32-hour (6-day) spans boundaries.

## C. CONSECUTIVE DAY (6th/7th) DETECTION

| Union | Counting Method | Rest Day Resets? | Travel Days Count? | Carries Across Weeks? |
|-------|----------------|-----------------|-------------------|----------------------|
| **IATSE** | Consecutive within workweek | Yes | Yes (min 4hr call ST, 8hr if travel >4hrs, 2nd consecutive travel = 2×) | Within defined workweek only |
| **SAG-AFTRA** | Producer designates 6 of 7 days | Yes | N/A | Within workweek |
| **DGA** | Any 5 of 7 consecutive | Yes (33hr rest = 1 day off, 50hr = full reset) | Yes | Past midnight on 6th = 7th day call |
| **PACT/BECTU** | Consecutive calendar days | Yes | Travel days are NEUTRAL (neither break nor add to streak) | Yes — but 8 consecutive days strictly prohibited |
| **Ver.di** | Weekly, 6th day +50% | Two consecutive rest days twice/month | N/A | Within week |
| **France** | Max 6 days exceptionally | 2 successive rest days after >6 days | N/A | Max 4 consecutive 6-day weeks |

### Key: BECTU travel days are NEUTRAL — don't break the count, don't add to it

## D. PAYSLIP GENERATION

**Payroll bureau generates payslips, NOT the production.**

| Bureau | Region | Format | Access |
|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Sargent-Disc** | UK | Native iOS/Android Payslip app + web | Push notifications, full archive 24/7 |
| **Cast & Crew** | US | MyCast&Crew portal (my.castandcrew.com) | Digital PDF paystubs, W-2s, YTD, pay history |
| **Entertainment Partners** | US/Global | My EP portal (my.ep.com) | View/download PDF, direct deposit, search across projects |

### Typical film payslip data:
- Employee: name, ID, department, job title, production
- Pay period dates, pay date
- Base rate (daily/weekly)
- Regular hours worked
- OT breakdown: 1.5× hours, 2× hours, golden time
- Penalties: meal, turnaround, forced call, 6th/7th day, night
- Allowances: per diem, mileage, kit, car, phone
- Gross total
- Deductions: tax, NI/FICA, union dues, pension, garnishments
- Employer contributions: NI 13.8%, pension, HP accrual
- YTD totals
- Net pay

## E. CONTRACTED HOURS VARIABILITY

**Yes — can change day-to-day based on day type:**

| Day Type | UK (BECTU) | Overtime After |
|----------|-----------|---------------|
| SWD (Standard) | 10 shooting hrs + 1hr lunch = 11hr day | After 10hrs |
| CWD (Continuous) | 9 shooting hrs, no formal break | After 9hrs |
| SCWD (Semi-Continuous) | 9.5 shooting hrs + 30min lunch | After 9.5hrs |
| Film MMP | SWD = 11hrs + 1hr lunch; CWD = 10hrs | After contracted hours |

**Who decides day type:**
- Default set in **Deal Memo**
- Daily changes via **1st AD** on call sheet
- Must be approved by **Line Producer** (cost implications)
- Non-shooting crew always SWD
- Certain depts (ADs, Costume, HMU, Locations) operate 10+1+1 structure

## F. RECEIPTS FOR ALLOWANCES

| Allowance | US (IRS) | UK (HMRC/BECTU) |
|-----------|---------|-----------------|
| **Per Diem** | No receipts if at/below federal rate (accountable plan) | No individual meal receipts if at scale rate; evidence of qualifying travel needed |
| **Mileage** | Mileage log required, no fuel receipts if at/below IRS rate ($0.70/mi 2025) | No receipt if at/below HMRC rate (£0.45/mi); some productions request mileage log |
| **Kit/Box Rental** | No receipts — deal memo is documentation. Must substantiate ownership if audited | No receipts — flat rate per deal memo |
| **Threshold** | $75+ for individual expenses (lodging). Per diem exempt | No explicit threshold. Scale rate payments exempt |

**Current evidence handling:** Paper declining. Digital upload via payroll apps (Sargent-Disc DPO, EP SmartStart, Wrapbook) increasingly standard. Email photos/scans still common on smaller productions.

| Union | First Meal Deadline | Duration | Deductible? | Penalty | Second Meal | Exemptions |
|-------|-------------------|----------|-------------|---------|-------------|------------|
| **IATSE (US)** | 6 hrs from call | 30 min | NDM (non-deductible, clock runs) | $7.50/$10/$12.50 escalating per 15-min. 20+ penalties/week triggers higher | 6 hrs from M1 return | None |
| **SAG-AFTRA (US)** | 6 hrs from call | 30 min (1hr if not catered) | NDM | $25/$35/$50 escalating (highest US) | 6 hrs from M1 return | None |
| **DGA (US)** | 6 hrs | 30 min | NDM | $75/violation flat | 6 hrs from M1 return | None |
| **Teamsters 399 (US)** | 6 hrs | 30 min | NDM | $35/violation flat | 6 hrs from M1 return | None |
| **PACT/BECTU (UK)** | 6 hrs from call | 30 min minimum | Deductible (clock stops) | £35/breach, OT rate in 15-min increments | After 5 hrs from M1 return | **CWD exempt** — no formal break, no penalty |
| **UK Equity** | 5.5 hrs from call | 1 hr | Deductible | Penalty rate | After 5 hrs | None |
| **MEAA (Australia)** | 5 hrs from call | 30 min | Deductible | Penalty rates per MPPA | After 5 hrs from M1 return | None |
| **Ver.di (Germany)** | Between 5th-6th hr | 45 min | Deductible | Quality penalty (warm meal required), not monetary. 2nd warm meal if >10hr day | After 6 hrs | None |
| **CCNPC (France)** | 6 hrs | 1 hr | Deductible | €18.56 meal allowance (repas) + €7.54 casse-croûte | After 6 hrs | None |
| **WGA (US)** | N/A | N/A | N/A | N/A | N/A | Writers are salaried/per-script — no production meal rules |
| **AFM (US)** | Per session agreement | Varies | Varies | Per session | Per session | Session-based, not daily |

### Key: UK meals STOP the clock. US meals (IATSE/SAG/DGA) are NDM — clock runs continuously.

## H. MINIMUM CALL RULES

| Union | Minimum Call | Day Players vs Weekly | Notes |
|-------|------------|----------------------|-------|
| **IATSE (US)** | 8 hours | Same | Paid for 8 hrs even if work < 8 hrs |

| **MEAA (Australia)** | 10 hours | Same | Standard engagement = 10hr day |
| **Ver.di (Germany)** | 8-10 hours | Per agreement | Minimum daily wage covers full day |

## I. PARTIAL DAY HANDLING

| Scenario | Treatment |
|----------|-----------|
| Sent home by production (rain, schedule change) | **Full day pay** — crew gets minimum call/daily rate |
| Goes home sick (own choice) | **Paid for hours worked** — but minimum call may still apply (union dependent) |
| Counts as work day for consecutive? | **Yes if called in** — even 1 hour of work = 1 work day for 6th/7th detection |
| Medical emergency | **Full day pay** usually — H&S provisions |

## J. NIGHT SHOOT / SPLIT DAY RULES

| Union | Definition | Which Day? | Special Rates | Turnaround |
|-------|-----------|-----------|---------------|------------|
| **IATSE** | Work past midnight | Belongs to date of CALL | Golden time from hr 14 | From company wrap + 1hr |
| **SAG-AFTRA** | Work past midnight | Belongs to date of call | No specific night premium | 12hr from personal dismissal |
| **DGA** | Past midnight on 6th day = 7th day call | Complex rules per agreement | Varies | 9hr from wrap |
| **PACT/BECTU** | Wrap past midnight | Belongs to date of call | £20 flat night premium (MMP §5.2) | 11hr from release |
| **Ver.di** | Work 22:00-06:00 | Belongs to date of call | +25% night markup | 11hr mandatory |
| **CCNPC (France)** | Work 22:00-06:00 | Belongs to date of call | +25% night markup | 11hr |

### Universal: Night shoot day "belongs to" the date of CREW CALL, not the date of release.

## K. LTD / CORPORATE WORKER TIMECARDS

| Territory | Do They Fill Timecards? | How Time is Tracked | Payment |
|-----------|------------------------|--------------------|---------| 
| **UK (Ltd)** | Usually NO formal timecard. Invoice for days worked | Production tracks via call sheets/back pages for cost reporting | Monthly invoice, production pays company |
| **US (Loan-out)** | YES — still fill timecards for union reporting | Same timecard system but payment goes to corp, not individual | Corp invoices or payroll processes to corp |
| **US (1099)** | Usually NO | Invoice-based, but production may request day log | 1099-NEC if $600+ |
| **Australia (Pty Ltd)** | Varies — some fill timecards, some invoice | Production tracks for SG (super guarantee) purposes | Invoice to company |
| **Germany (GmbH)** | NO | Invoice-based | Invoice |
| **France (Auto-entrepreneur)** | NO | Invoice-based | Invoice |

### Key: US Loan-outs still use the timecard system (union requirement). UK Ltd companies typically invoice.
