## Key Questions to Resolve in Tests

1. **OT stacking:** 6th day + night + wrap OT — multipliers compound or highest wins?
2. **Allowance fringeability:** Kit, per diem, mileage — subject to HP/NIC/pension?
3. **Penalty fringeability:** Meal/BTA penalties subject to employer fringes?
4. **All-In scope:** Configurable per deal memo what's absorbed
5. **HP back-calc:** base = inclusive_rate / 1.1207
6. **Buyout fringeability:** Usage rights fringeable under PAYE?
7. **Rest day vs consecutive:** Which premium governs when both apply?
8. **Weekend turnaround:** 34-36hr minimum vs 11hr daily?
9. **Partial week proration:** 3/5ths or full week?
10. **Sick day + consecutive count:** Breaks the counter or not?
